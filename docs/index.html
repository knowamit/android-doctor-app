The Real Reasons

Your phone didn't get slow by accident.

  • 01
    NAND flash degradation. Storage chips physically wear out after thousands of write cycles. Read/write speeds drop 30-50%. A factory reset doesn't repair silicon.
  • 02
    Battery-driven CPU throttling. After 300-500 charge cycles, internal resistance rises. Your CPU throttles itself by 30-50% to prevent overheating.
  • 03
    Manufacturer bloatware. Samsung ships 47 pre-installed apps. Xiaomi's ad framework runs constantly. Facebook Services is pre-installed on most phones. All consuming RAM, CPU, battery.
  • 04
    Software inflation. Apps you re-install are 3-5x larger than when your phone was new. Google Play Services alone uses 400MB+ of RAM.
  • 05
    Corporate negligence. Samsung throttled 10,000+ apps while exempting benchmarks ($58M settlement). Apple throttled iPhones without disclosure ($500M settlement).

"Cleaner apps on the Play Store show fake scans and serve ads. They are the bloatware."
